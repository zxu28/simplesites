About Science

Science is a systematic enterprise that builds and organizes knowledge in the form of testable explanations and predictions about the universe. Through observation, experimentation, and analysis, scientists uncover the fundamental principles that govern our world.

From understanding the building blocks of matter to exploring the mysteries of space, science continues to push the boundaries of human knowledge and innovation.
